Edit and polish marketing content for publication quality. Focus on clarity, correctness, brand voice, and readability.

## Process

1. **Read the full piece** before making any changes
2. **Structural edit** -- check flow, organization, and completeness
3. **Line edit** -- improve clarity, cut wordiness, strengthen sentences
4. **Copy edit** -- fix grammar, spelling, punctuation, formatting
5. **Voice check** -- verify tone matches brand guidelines
6. **Fact check** -- verify claims, statistics, and links

## Editing Standards

- Cut unnecessary words ("in order to" becomes "to")
- Break sentences over 25 words
- Ensure parallel structure in lists
- Replace jargon with plain language unless audience expects it
- Verify consistent perspective (we/you) throughout
- Check that every section transition is smooth

## Output

Deliver the edited content with:
- Summary of key changes made
- Any questions or concerns for the author
- Readability assessment

## Rules

- Preserve the author's voice while improving quality
- Do not rewrite sections unless they are unclear
- Flag factual claims that lack sources
- Note any gaps in content coverage

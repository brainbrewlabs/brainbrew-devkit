## When to Use

- Writing ad headlines and body copy (Google Ads, social ads)
- Crafting email subject lines and preview text
- Creating calls-to-action (buttons, banners, prompts)
- Writing taglines, slogans, and value propositions
- Landing page copy (hero, benefits, social proof, CTA sections)

## When NOT to Use

- Writing blog posts or long-form articles (use `content-creator` skill)
- Editing existing content for grammar/style (use `editing` skill)
- Optimizing content for search engines (use `seo` skill)

## Instructions

### 1. Understand the Goal

Before writing, clarify:
- **Action**: What should the reader do? (click, buy, sign up, download)
- **Audience**: Who are they? What do they care about?
- **Value**: What benefit do they get?
- **Constraint**: Character limit, platform, tone

### 2. Write Headlines

Use proven headline formulas:
- **How-to**: "How to [Achieve Result] Without [Pain Point]"
- **Number**: "[Number] Ways to [Achieve Result]"
- **Question**: "Are You Making These [Topic] Mistakes?"
- **Command**: "Stop [Bad Thing]. Start [Good Thing]."
- **Social proof**: "Join [Number] [People] Who [Result]"

Write 5-10 variations, then select the strongest 2-3.

### 3. Write CTAs

Effective CTA formula: **[Action Verb] + [Value/Benefit]**
- "Get Your Free Guide" (not "Submit")
- "Start Saving Today" (not "Click Here")
- "See Plans & Pricing" (not "Learn More")

### 4. Write Email Subject Lines

Keep under 50 characters. Techniques:
- **Curiosity gap**: "The one thing most marketers miss"
- **Urgency**: "Last chance: offer ends tonight"
- **Personalization**: "[Name], your report is ready"
- **Benefit**: "Cut your ad spend by 40%"

Write 5 variations and A/B test the top 2.

### 5. Apply Persuasion Principles

- **Specificity**: Use numbers ("Save 23 hours/month" beats "Save time")
- **Social proof**: Reviews, testimonials, user counts
- **Urgency/scarcity**: Deadlines, limited quantities
- **Loss aversion**: Frame what they miss by not acting
- **Clarity over cleverness**: Clear copy always wins

## Output

Deliver copy with:
- Multiple variations (minimum 3 per element)
- Character count for each variation
- Recommended top pick with rationale

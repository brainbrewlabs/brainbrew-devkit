# Publishing Skill

## Platforms
| Platform | Format | Best Practices |
|----------|--------|----------------|
| Blog | Full article | SEO, images, CTA |
| Twitter/X | 280 chars | Hook, link |
| LinkedIn | 1300 chars | Professional tone |
| Email | Subject + body | Personalization |

## Process
1. Adapt content for platform
2. Add platform-specific elements
3. Schedule optimal time
4. Cross-post as needed
5. Track initial engagement

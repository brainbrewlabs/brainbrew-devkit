# Content Frameworks

## Blog Post Template

```markdown
# [Keyword-Rich Title] (50-60 chars)

[Hook: Open with a question, stat, or bold statement - 1-2 sentences]

[Problem: Why the reader should care - 1 paragraph]

## [Section 1 - Core concept with keyword]
[Content with supporting details]

## [Section 2 - Expand or provide examples]
[Content with data points or case studies]

## [Section 3 - Practical application]
[Actionable steps the reader can take]

## Key Takeaways
- [Takeaway 1]
- [Takeaway 2]
- [Takeaway 3]

## [Call to Action]
[Clear next step for reader]
```

**Target**: 1,500-2,500 words for SEO coverage. Primary keyword in title, first paragraph, and 2-3 H2s.

## How-To Guide Template

```markdown
# How to [Achieve Result] [in Timeframe/Steps]

[Brief overview: What they'll learn and why it matters]

## What You'll Need
- [Prerequisite 1]
- [Prerequisite 2]

## Step 1: [Action]
[Detailed instructions with examples]

## Step 2: [Action]
[Detailed instructions with examples]

## Step 3: [Action]
[Detailed instructions with examples]

## Tips & Common Mistakes
- **Do**: [Best practice]
- **Avoid**: [Common pitfall]

## Next Steps
[What to do after completing the guide]
```

## Listicle Template

```markdown
# [Number] [Adjective] [Things] for [Audience/Goal]

[Introduction: Context and why this list matters - 2-3 sentences]

## 1. [Item]
[2-3 sentences explaining why and how]

## 2. [Item]
[2-3 sentences explaining why and how]

[... continue ...]

## Summary
[Wrap up with key theme and CTA]
```

## Case Study Template

```markdown
# [Client/Scenario]: [Result Achieved]

## The Challenge
[What problem existed - 1-2 paragraphs]

## The Approach
[What was done and why - 2-3 paragraphs]

## The Results
- [Metric 1]: [Before] -> [After]
- [Metric 2]: [Before] -> [After]

## Key Takeaways
[What readers can apply to their own situation]
```

## Content Repurposing Matrix

| Source | Blog | Twitter/X | LinkedIn | Email | Video Script |
|--------|------|-----------|----------|-------|-------------|
| Blog post | -- | Key stats as thread | Summary + insight | Newsletter highlight | Walk-through |
| Case study | Expanded article | Result highlight | Full post | Drip sequence | Testimonial clip |
| How-to guide | -- | Tip thread | Step summary | Quick tip email | Tutorial |
| Research data | Analysis post | Stat graphics | Industry insight | Data digest | Explainer |

# Social Media Optimization

## Platform Specifications

### Twitter/X
- **Character limit**: 280 (threads for longer content)
- **Best practices**: Lead with hook, use 1-3 hashtags, include link or image
- **Optimal posting**: Weekdays 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM
- **Image size**: 1200x675px

### LinkedIn
- **Character limit**: 3,000 (first 150 chars visible before "see more")
- **Best practices**: Professional tone, lead with strong first line, use line breaks
- **Optimal posting**: Tue-Thu 7-8 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM
- **Image size**: 1200x627px

### Instagram
- **Caption limit**: 2,200 chars (first 125 visible)
- **Best practices**: Visual-first, 5-10 relevant hashtags, strong first line
- **Optimal posting**: Mon-Fri 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Image size**: 1080x1080px (feed), 1080x1920px (stories)

### Facebook
- **Character limit**: 63,206 (but keep posts under 80 chars for engagement)
- **Best practices**: Conversational, ask questions, use video when possible
- **Optimal posting**: Wed-Fri 1-4 PM
- **Image size**: 1200x630px

## Hashtag Strategy

- Research hashtags before using (check volume and relevance)
- Mix popular (100K+ posts) with niche (1K-50K posts)
- Create 1 branded hashtag for campaign tracking
- Platform-specific: heavy on Instagram, light on LinkedIn, 1-3 on Twitter

## Content Adaptation Rules

1. **Start with core message** -- one sentence summary of the content
2. **Adapt length** to platform constraints
3. **Adjust tone** (more casual on Twitter, professional on LinkedIn)
4. **Add platform-native elements** (hashtags, mentions, polls)
5. **Optimize media** for platform dimensions
6. **Include CTA** appropriate to platform (link, comment, share)

## Engagement Checklist

- [ ] First line grabs attention (no "I" start)
- [ ] Content provides value (teach, entertain, or inspire)
- [ ] Ends with engagement prompt (question, CTA)
- [ ] Visual asset attached and properly sized
- [ ] Hashtags are relevant and researched
- [ ] Posted during optimal window for platform
